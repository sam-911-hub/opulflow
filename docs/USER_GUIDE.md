# OpulFlow User Guide

## Introduction

Welcome to OpulFlow, the pay-as-you-go sales intelligence platform. This guide will help you get started with OpulFlow and make the most of its features.

## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit [opulflow.com](https://opulflow.com)
2. Click on "Get Started Free" or "Register"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click "Create Account"

### Logging In

1. Visit [opulflow.com](https://opulflow.com)
2. Click on "Login"
3. Enter your email and password
4. Click "Sign in"

## Dashboard Overview

The OpulFlow dashboard is your central hub for accessing all features. Here's what you'll find:

- **Overview**: Quick access to your credits, recent activity, and key metrics
- **Leads**: Manage and track your leads

- **Help**: Get help and support

## Credit System

OpulFlow uses a credit-based system that allows you to pay only for what you use:

### Purchasing Credits

1. Go to the "Credits" tab in your dashboard
2. Choose a credit package or create a custom package
3. Select your payment method (PayPal or M-PESA)
4. Complete the payment

### Credit Usage

Different services consume different amounts of credits:

- **Lead Lookup**: 1 credit per lead
- **Company Enrichment**: 1 credit per company
- **Email Verification**: 1 credit per email
- **AI Email Generation**: 1 credit per email
- **Workflow Execution**: 1 credit per run

### Credit Expiration

Credits expire 90 days after purchase. You can view your credit expiration dates in the "Credits" tab.

1. Go to the "Leads" tab

## FAQ

### How do credits work?

Credits are used to access various services on the platform. Each service costs a specific number of credits. Credits expire 90 days after purchase.

### Can I get a refund?

We do not offer refunds for used credits. Unused credits expire after 90 days and are non-refundable.

### How do I cancel my account?

You can cancel your account at any time by going to Settings > Account > Cancel Account. Since OpulFlow uses a pay-as-you-go model, there are no subscriptions to cancel.

### How do I upgrade to Pro?

Go to the "Credits" tab and click "Upgrade to Pro" to access additional features and higher usage limits.
